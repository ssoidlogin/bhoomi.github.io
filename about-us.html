About Us

Welcome to Bhoomi Karnataka, your dedicated informational resource for navigating the digital land records system of Karnataka. Our mission is to simplify complex government processes and provide citizens with clear, step-by-step guidance on managing their property documents online.

Our Vision

In an era of digital transformation, we believe that every landowner and farmer in Karnataka should have easy access to their land records without the need for multiple visits to government offices. We aim to bridge the information gap by providing a user-friendly source to the official Bhoomi services.

What We Provide

Through our platform, we offer detailed insights on:

  • RTC (Pahani) Retrieval: How to view and download your Record of Rights, Tenancy, and Crops.
  • Mutation Tracking: Understanding the process of ownership transfer and checking application status.
  • Land Mapping: Guides on using Revenue Maps and the Dishaank App for GPS-based survey identification.
  • Legal Verification: Instructions on i-RTC downloads and XML verification to ensure document authenticity.

Why Use Our Service?

While the official Bhoomi portal is a powerful tool, it can sometimes be overwhelming for first-time users. We break down technical terms like Surnoc, Hissa, and Mutation Register into simple language. Our content is updated for 2026 to reflect the latest service charges and portal features.
